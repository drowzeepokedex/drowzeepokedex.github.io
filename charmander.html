Biology

Charmander is a bipedal, reptilian Pokémon with a flame burning at the tip of its tail. The flame reflects its health and mood, flaring brightly when it's strong and weakly when it's tired. It prefers warm places and can often be seen basking in the sun or seeking heat sources when cold.
