Biology

Venusaur is a large, quadrupedal Pokémon with a massive pink flower blooming on its back. This flower is a result of Ivysaur's bud fully maturing and is supported by a thick stem and broad leaves. Venusaur absorbs sunlight to convert into energy, often resting in sunny spots to stay energized. The aroma of its flower is said to calm emotions and affect its surroundings.
