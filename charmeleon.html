Biology

Charmeleon is a bipedal, reptilian Pokémon with orange skin and a flame at the tip of its tail. It is the evolved form of Charmander and becomes more aggressive and hot-headed as it matures. It has the ability to unleash intense flames from its body when enraged, and the flame on its tail burns hotter when it is angry. As it evolves into Charizard, it gains the ability to fly and becomes a powerful force in battle.
